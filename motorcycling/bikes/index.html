bikes I had

A few pictures of the different bikes I owned over the years.

Ducati 748R. Such a beautiful motorcycle… Loved that bike but it was too fragile for me. Beware of buying R (for Racing) models of Ducatis…

This pretty yellow Supersport was my first Ducati. Fast and reliable bike, so much fun on the track!

Ducati SS900ie

This GasGas 250 EC (2-stroke) was my first enduro bike. Easy and stable with a snappy engine.

This nice Yamaha YZ250F was my first step into 4-stroke off-road (and motocross)

Yamaha YZ250F

Yamaha WR250F. Raced a lot of enduros with that WRF. Awesome bike with just enough power.
