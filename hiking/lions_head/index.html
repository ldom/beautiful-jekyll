Lion's Head, Bruce Trail, Ontario

The Lion’s Head section of the Bruce Trail is a very nice 2-day hike and one of the rare places where you can camp (officially) on the Bruce Trail.

I passed over Lion’s Head a couple of times through my flights. Interesting how a small area from above takes 1 or 2 days to hike on foot…
