La Cloche Silhouette, Killarney, Ontario

La Cloche Silhouette is a 90-km loop in the Killarney Provincial Park.

The hike is over rough terrain. Lots of rocks. It goes up and down all the time. I did it in 4 days, door-to-door from Toronto, but reasonably I should have taken one more day. Some days I hiked more than 12 hours and the weather was quite warm.

I flew to the little Killarney aerodrome with the Cirrus and then got a lift to the park.

(No guessing which route I chose.)

Pictures in chronological order. I did the loop clock-wise.
